Kinetic Sculpture Race

21 May 2013

This was one of my first major projects. I entered in a competition to build a human-powered vehicle that could traverse land, sand, and water for my high school senior project. The Kinetic Sculpture Race Grand Championship has been an annual tradition in Arcata, CA since 1969, in which dozens of DIY engineers build vehicles and race eachother over 3 days and 42 miles in Humboldt County.
