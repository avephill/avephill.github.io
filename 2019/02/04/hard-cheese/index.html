Monterey Jack Cheese

4 Feb 2019

I made hard cheese for a lab meeting one term. It was pretty straight forward and turned out edible. I used this recipe.

The general steps consisted of

  1. Heat the milk + rennet + bacteria culture and separate curds from whey

  2. Figure out someway to press the curds into a mold

  3. Cover in wax and let sit for weeks/months
